Eliminating the middleman and bringing you the best daily fuel price!

In a deregulated fuel market, supply and demand should determine the price you pay. However, oil majors' monopolised structures and procedures and an extensive agent network are inflating the fuel price.

Your solution – the Bauzer platform!

The Bauzer platform is an electronic, real-time, web-based fuel trading solution. The platform lists the best available daily price at the registered collection points, so you know the best fuel price at any given time. We also vet the supplier and quality of supply. For your convenience, we manage the entire purchase transaction for you.  

Suppliers in turn can bid for your business, for the best possible bulk volume discount. You get the best price and suppliers can sell direct to the market. The middleman is removed – meaning more money in your pocket! 

There is no charge to register and use the platform – you simply pay the quoted price. We can also arrange transport and delivery of the fuel for you.

With the Bauzer Platform, you can buy and trade in a free market and feel confident you are getting the best possible fuel price!
